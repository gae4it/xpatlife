## Key Takeaways

1. **Start early** - Immigration processes can take months
2. **Research your visa type** - Requirements vary significantly
3. **Budget carefully** - Initial costs are higher than monthly expenses
4. **Prepare documents** - Have everything certified and translated
5. **Stay informed** - Immigration rules can change

## Next Steps

Once you've completed your planning:

1. Gather and prepare all required documents
2. Apply for your visa or residence permit
3. Arrange temporary accommodation for your arrival
4. Set up initial finances (bank account, funds transfer)
5. Move on to [Arrival and First Steps](/guide/arrival-and-first-steps)
